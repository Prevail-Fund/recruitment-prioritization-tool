Potential levels of engagement
For levels 1–3, Prevail plays a supporting role — the partner owns the outcome and can/should use other recruitment resources. At level 4, Prevail takes on more responsibility for the outcome.
Level 1
  • Share existing candidates + make intros
Level 2
  • Share existing candidates
  • Source (networking & online)
  • Potentially initial screening
Level 3
  • Share existing candidates
  • Source, screen, full interviews
  • Full process & evaluation support
Level 4
  • Full end-to-end responsibility
  • Potentially embed recruiter
