Levels of engagement
Level 1
  • Share existing candidates + make intros
Level 2
  • Share existing candidates
  • Source (networking & online)
  • Potentially initial screening
Level 3
  • Share existing candidates
  • Source, screen, full interviews
  • Full process & evaluation support
Level 4
  • Full end-to-end responsibility
  • Potentially embed recruiter
For levels 1–3, we are explicit that Prevail plays a supporting role only — the partner owns the outcome and shouldn't rely solely on Prevail. At level 4, Prevail takes on more responsibility for the outcome.

Prioritization criteria

Consider these criteria to help inform how/whether we should provide recruitment support. Note that this is directional only to guide good judgment - there will always be exceptions.

Our ability to succeed
Do we have recruiter capacity? Have we hired a similar role before? Do we have a strong network or existing pipeline?
When & how we'd engage
If high, lean in depending on the other three factors.

If low, stick to level 1 unless the strategic benefit is high, in which case proceed if it helps us to learn. If proceeding, reduce risk by setting clear expectations — tell partners what we can and can't do, make sure they're not relying on us, and ensure they have other options.
